Liberty Park Safety!

/spreading awareness for public health

Salt Lake City, Utah

Background

History

Founded in 1881, Liberty Park is one of the only green spaces in downtown SLC, UT. Unfortunately, due to various reasons, many people do not feel completely safe visiting the park.

Stakeholders

Liberty Park attracts visitors from all over the world. The multi-use bike path is heavily trafficked. All of these people as well as community members residing in the area and business owners are affected by saftey issues in the park.

Goals

This is a public health campaign with a goal of spreading awareness. My hope is that informing the community about safety concerns and offering safety tips will imrpove the overall health of our community.
